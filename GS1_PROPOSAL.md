## What the Platform Does

### 1. AI-Powered Assessments from Your Documents

GS1 already has training materials — manuals, standards documents, guides, slide decks. Instead of writing quiz questions from scratch, your team simply uploads a PDF. The platform reads the document and generates a set of questions and answers automatically, covering the key concepts inside.

Your content team reviews the suggestions, removes or edits any that miss the mark, and publishes the assessment — typically in minutes rather than days.

Learners then answer these questions as part of their course. If they get something wrong, the platform points them back to the exact section of the original document where the answer is explained. Passing the assessment unlocks the next section of the course and, ultimately, their certificate.

**What this means for GS1:**
- Dramatically less time spent authoring course materials
- Consistent assessment quality across all programmes
- Existing documents become learning assets immediately

---

### 2. Live Classes Inside the Platform

GS1 trainers can schedule live virtual sessions directly in the platform. Enrolled learners see the upcoming class on their course dashboard, receive automatic reminders before it starts, and join with a single click — without ever leaving the platform or needing a separate video call link.

During the session, trainers can present, share their screen, and interact with participants. Sessions can be recorded. When a session ends, the recording is automatically saved and made available to enrolled learners who could not attend live.

Attendance is tracked automatically — GS1 can see who joined each session and for how long.

**What this means for GS1:**
- No more sharing separate Zoom or Teams links with learners
- Attendance records maintained without manual tracking
- Missed sessions covered by automatic recordings

---

### 3. Certificates That Mean Something

Learners who complete a course and pass the assessments receive a branded GS1 certificate automatically — no manual processing required.

Each certificate carries a unique reference number and GS1's branding, including the issuing body (e.g. "GS1 Kenya"), the course title, the signatory, and the date of completion.

Certificates can be shared with employers and verified online. Anyone with the certificate's reference number can visit a public verification page and confirm that the credential is genuine — without logging in or contacting GS1 directly.

**What this means for GS1:**
- Certificates issued the moment a learner qualifies — no delays
- Employers can verify credentials instantly, adding real value to the qualification
- Zero manual effort from GS1 staff

---

### 4. Notifications That Keep Learners Engaged

Learners receive timely, relevant updates through three channels: in-app alerts, email, and SMS. This covers the moments that matter most:

- Welcome message when they register
- Confirmation when their payment goes through
- Reminder 15 minutes before a live class starts
- Result when they complete an assessment
- Notification — and a download link — when their certificate is ready

The in-app notification centre gives learners a running history of all their updates in one place, accessible whenever they log in.

**What this means for GS1:**
- Learners stay on track without needing to chase support
- Lower drop-off rates through timely reminders
- SMS coverage ensures learners on mobile networks don't miss critical updates

## The Learner Experience, End to End

1. A learner registers on the platform and receives a welcome email.
2. They browse GS1's course catalogue and enrol, paying by card or mobile money.
3. They work through video lectures at their own pace, with their progress saved automatically.
4. At the end of each section, they complete a short AI-generated quiz based on the course materials.
5. They join a scheduled live session with a GS1 trainer, or watch the recording if they miss it.
6. Once they complete all sections and pass all assessments, their certificate is generated and sent to them automatically.
7. They share their certificate with their employer, who verifies it online in seconds.

## The GS1 Staff Experience

GS1 administrators and trainers manage everything from a dedicated admin area:

- **Upload and publish courses** — add video lectures, upload PDFs, review AI-generated questions, and set passing scores.
- **Schedule live sessions** — create a class, set the date and time, and the platform handles invitations and reminders.
- **Monitor learner progress** — see who is enrolled, how far they have progressed, and which assessments they have passed or failed.
- **View attendance records** — see who attended each live session and for how long.
- **Track revenue and enrolments** — a real-time dashboard shows registrations, payments, and completion rates.

## Security and Data Protection

Learner data is handled responsibly and in line with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019. Key protections include:

- Course content is only accessible to learners who have enrolled and paid — enforced on the server, not just in the interface.
- Payment card details are never stored on the platform; payments are processed through Paystack, a PCI-compliant provider.
- Documents uploaded for AI processing do not include any learner personal information.
- Certificates are protected by unique reference numbers and served through secure, time-limited links.
- The platform is hosted on infrastructure based in Johannesburg, reducing data latency for African users and keeping data within the region.
